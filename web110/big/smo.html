Social Media Optimization (SMO)

WHAT IS SMO?

Social Media Optimization (SMO) is an incredibly important part of any business's web presence today. We know Social Media is about sites like Faceboook, Twitter, Instagram, or LinkedIn, but SMO is more than just having pages for your business. Optimizing your website and your content to encourage users to interact and share it across platforms is an important part. To really make the most of social media, you need to engage with your customers in strategically creative ways, while also making the experience useful for them in some way - from entertaining and memorable, to useful and helpful. You want to have curated content and great useability to better engage with your customers, and make your site easy to link to, more likely to be seen in search engines, and higher inclusion in related blogs and other media.

WHY IS SMO IMPORTANT?

The reasons for this are actually pretty straight forward. SMO can help increase traffic to your site, and a good interactive site will keep users there, and keep them coming back. Engaging with your customers in an intelligent and respectful way will build brand loyalty, and will encourage customers to share your site, helping do your marketing for you.

"how many times have you seen someone you know share a link to Facebook only to have the text cut off, garbled, or otherwise unclear? How often have you seen a tweet that’s missing the second half of the headline?"
- Jessica Bryant, Seattlewebdesign.com1

A good social media optimized site can establish you as an authority in your industry, gives you better customer insight, and increases your brand awareness. This all helps with the bottom line. According to Samuels with Sproutsocial, “When consumers follow brands on social, 67% say they are more likely to increase their spending with that brand.” (Samuels, Sproutsocial)2

TRENDS AND TACTICS:

In order to increase your SMO, there are a variety of tactics to use. According to Alex Uifalean at Lumen5.com3, some of the popular trends for 2019 are:

  • Being video friendly: high quality, useful, and short
  • Participating in groups: interacting with the community and showing expertise
  • Blog posts: sharing important, useful info with your community
  • Hashtags: using up to 11 hashtags to increase visibility on posts
  • Frequency of posting: at least daily, site depending, to keep visibility
  • Have quality visual content: pairing graphics with text increases retention of the info about your business

This is just the tip of the iceberg. There are many tools out there as well to help track your SMO performance, user interactions, and tools to make sharing your content easier and easier for your users.
