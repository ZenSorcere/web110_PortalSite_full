Welcome!

Welome to my BIG website, my final project for my online WEB110 Web Authoring 1 class, Summer quarter of 2019, taught by Sara Newman. This final project is designed to present examples of the various aspects of what we learned this quarter, and introduced us to additional aspects of Web development with HMTL and CSS.

In the menu nav above are the following pages:

  • Web110 - a link to my introduction site, with information about myself and background, a table explaining aspects of various HTML elements, some resource links used, and a simple contact form.
  • Web Research - We were assigned to research three topics, cite our sources, and provide an overview of the topics listed below:
    • Web Accessibility
    • Social Media Optimization (SMO)
    • Forms
  • Lightbox II Gallery - we learned how to add a responsive photo gallery to our site along with using the <figure> and <figcapture> elements.
  • Google Tools - we learned how to embed various elements frequently found on websites, listed below:
    • SEO YouTube video
    • Google Calendar
    • Google Map
  • Contact Form - a more involved Contact Form that the one seen in the Web110 link, utilizing additional Form aspects.
